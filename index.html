About Me

My name is Michael Anderson. I was born in the United States and live with my family in Utah. I work for the Utah Department of Transportation as an Inspector and focus my efforts on helping people get home safely on the roads. I adore my family and enjoy playing games and watching movies with them. I welcome a challenge and love overcoming it with hard work and persistance.

Utah is known for great desert beauty, towering mountains, and the Great Salt Lake. Located in the rocky mountains, Utah is also well known for having some of the greatest snow on earth, which was seen world wide during the 2002 Winter Olympics. Utah is expecting more of the Olymics in 2034 and is expected to more than triple its current population within the next 10 years.
